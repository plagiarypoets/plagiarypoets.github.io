Like as the gentle hart it selfe bewraies,
Like as the gentle hart it selfe bewraies,
In doing gentle deeds with franck delight,
In doing gentle deeds with franck delight,
Even so the baser minde it selfe displaies,
In cancred mallice,
In cancred mallice and revengeful spight,
In cancred mallice and revengeful spight,
Cancred mallice and revengeful spight.

"Alma novem celebres peperit Rhedycina Poetas,
Bubb, Grubb, Stubbe, Crabb, Trapp, Young, Carey, Tickel, Evans."

Cowley, Prior, Waller, Spenser, Milton, Dryden, et Gray,
Aeterum matri sunt tibi, Granta, decus.

I, nunc, et pulchras arces, Rhedycina, recense,
Congestu lapidum non sumus, esto, pares.
